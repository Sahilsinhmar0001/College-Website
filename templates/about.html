Mission

Vision

To be a nationally recognized institution for academic excellence, impactful research, and meaningful community partnerships.

A short history

  1. 1965 — Founded as a liberal arts college with three departments.
  2. 1988 — Expanded programs to include engineering and applied sciences.
  3. 2005 — Established industry partnerships and an on-campus research center.
  4. 2018 — Launched postgraduate and professional development programs.
